Welcome to Makhado FM

Makhado FM 107.3 is more than just a radio station; it's a platform for our voices, our culture, and our community. Based in the vibrant town of Louis Trichardt, we serve the entire Makhado Local Municipality and beyond.

We broadcast in a rich tapestry of languages, reflecting our diverse heritage: Venda, Tsonga, Sepedi, Afrikaans, and English. Our goal is to inform, entertain, and inspire every listener, from the youth to our elders.
